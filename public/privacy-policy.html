Privacy Policy

Short version: we collect as little as possible, we don't sell anything, and we don't track you around the web.

If you just read articles (no account)

We don't collect anything. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. You're invisible to us.

If you create an account

We store your email address, your display name, and a hashed password (we never see your actual password). That's it. We don't ask for your real name, phone number, location, or anything else.

Your comments and likes are stored and linked to your account so they show up on articles.

Third-party services we use

Supabase — stores the database (your account, comments, articles). Cloudflare Pages — serves the website. Google Fonts — loads the fonts. That's all. None of these are given your personal data for advertising purposes.

Want your data deleted?

Sign in and delete your account, or contact us and we'll do it. Your comments and likes go with it.

Emails

We only send emails if Supabase needs to verify your account or reset your password. No newsletters, no marketing.
